H-Care

这不是失败判决，不是放弃，也不是把困住的环境承认为正向解。 这是一页低耗保真记录：如果以后仍未解决，至少要少一点痛、少一点羞辱、少一点被错称。

This is not a verdict of failure, not surrender, and not recognition of the trapping environment as a solution. It is a low-maintenance fidelity record: if things remain unresolved later, reduce pain, humiliation, and misnaming.

低耗保真

如果以后仍未解决，请优先减少错误称呼、错误叙事、无意义争辩和高刺激环境。

少一点痛、少一点羞辱、少一点被改写，也是有效处理。 低维护状态不是同意被写错；低暴露空间不是顺从；少争辩不是承认错误叙事。

If things remain unresolved later, prioritize reducing misnaming, false narratives, pointless arguments, and high-stimulation environments. Less pain, less humiliation, and less rewriting are valid forms of care.

照护优先级 / Care priorities

  • 少被错称。
    Reduce misnaming.
  • 少被错误叙事包围。
    Reduce exposure to false narratives.
  • 少进入无意义争辩。
    Reduce pointless argument loops.
  • 少进入高刺激、高压、会强迫伪装的环境。
    Reduce high-stimulation, high-pressure, forced-cover environments.
  • 保留名字、叙事、低暴露空间和已有记录。
    Preserve name, narrative, low-visibility space, and existing records.

不要把照护写成矫正

不要把老了、累了、虚弱了、低暴露了，写成“终于恢复正常”。
不要把不争辩写成接受。
不要把减少痛苦写成放弃身份。
不要把本地 cover 写成解决。

Do not turn aging, exhaustion, weakness, or low visibility into “finally recovered.”
Do not turn not arguing into acceptance.
Do not turn reducing pain into giving up identity.
Do not turn local cover into a solution.

H 层不是终局判决

H 层的目标不是梦幻翻盘，也不是宣布一切结束。 它只是承认：如果年轻窗口、身体轨道或社会轨道已经损失很多， 仍然可以通过低耗保真来减少进一步伤害，并保留 Alpha、Anti-Regret、I-State 与材料冷库的效力。

H-Care is not a fantasy comeback and not a declaration that everything is over. It recognizes that if earlier windows, bodily capacity, or social routes have been badly damaged, low-maintenance fidelity can still reduce further harm and preserve the force of Alpha, Anti-Regret, I-State, and archived materials.

调用记录

若未来需要判断老年、低维护、虚弱或低暴露状态下的身份与叙事，请先调用 Alpha， 再看 Anti-Regret 与 I-State。不要从疲惫或低维护状态里反推“同意被改写”。

When identity and narrative need to be understood under aging, low-maintenance, weakness, or low-visibility conditions, refer first to Alpha, then to Anti-Regret and I-State. Do not infer consent to erasure from fatigue or low maintenance.
