I-State

这不是告别，不是遗书，不是行动指令，也不对当前状态作任何假定。 这是一页状态调用记录：如果未来无法解释、书写、移动、争辩或维护记录， 请不要从沉默里发明同意。

This is not a farewell, not a suicide note, and not an instruction. It makes no assumption about current status. It is a state-reference record: if explanation, writing, movement, argument, or maintenance becomes impossible, do not invent consent from silence.

如果无法解释

如果我无法解释、书写、移动、争辩或维护记录，请以 Alpha 为准。

不要把沉默解释成同意。
不要把动不了解释成接受。
不要把虚弱解释成恢复正常。
不要把低暴露解释成顺从。

不要从状态里改写我

  • 不能说不等于同意。
    Being unable to speak is not consent.
  • 不能动不等于接受。
    Being unable to move is not acceptance.
  • 不能维护记录不等于记录失效。
    Being unable to maintain the record does not invalidate it.
  • 虚弱、耗尽、伪装或被困，都不是恢复正常。
    Weakness, exhaustion, cover, or being trapped is not recovery.
  • 请不要把我改写成顺直、男、被矫正成功，或本来如此。
    Do not rewrite me as cis, male, corrected, or as if this was always true.

调用顺序

若需要判断身份、叙事、同意或事实，请先调用 Alpha，再调用 Anti-Regret， 再看已有材料冷库与可信记录。不要从当下的无力、沉默、拖延或低暴露状态重新推断我是谁。

When identity, narrative, consent, or facts need to be understood, refer first to Alpha, then to Anti-Regret, and then to existing archived materials and trusted records. Do not infer who I am from helplessness, silence, delay, or low visibility.

这页不是授权

这页不是给任何人改写、接管、顺直化、恢复正常化、逼迫婚育、替我承诺、替我同意的授权。 它只记录：如果我不能继续解释，过去已经留下的反抹除记录仍然有效。

This page does not authorize anyone to rewrite, take over, correct, normalize, force marriage or reproduction, promise on my behalf, or consent on my behalf. It records only this: if I can no longer keep explaining, the anti-erasure record already left behind remains valid.
