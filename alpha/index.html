Core statement

我没有变回去。
我没有同意被顺直化。
我不是“恢复正常”。
如果我沉默、低暴露、拖延、伪装或被困住， 请不要把那解释成接受。

I have not changed back.
I have not consented to being rewritten as cis or straight.
I am not “recovered” into a false normal.
If I am silent, low-visible, delayed, covered, or trapped, do not read that as acceptance.

不许改写 / Do not rewrite this

  • 沉默不是同意。
    Silence is not consent.
  • 低暴露不是投降。
    Low visibility is not surrender.
  • 生存伪装不是顺直化成功。
    Survival cover is not correction.
  • 被困住不是自愿受苦。
    Being trapped is not voluntary suffering.
  • 没有出口不等于认同困住我的系统。
    Lack of an exit does not mean agreement with the system that trapped me.

为什么这个页面存在 / Why this page exists

这个页面是为了完备性而存在。若某些路线长期失败，若某些制度不承认， 若某些环境只提供压制、伪装和拖延，这里仍然记录一件事： 我没有同意被改写成另一个人。

This page exists for completeness. If formal routes fail for a long time, if systems refuse recognition, if the environment only allows suppression, cover, and delay, this record still states one fact: I did not consent to being rewritten as someone else.

如何记住 / How to remember

请按我认可的身份、性别与叙述来记住我。 不要把等待说成接受，不要把被困说成选择， 不要把生存说成“想通了”“恢复正常了”或“本来就是那样”。

Please remember me by the identity, gender, and narrative I choose. Do not turn waiting into acceptance, being trapped into choice, or survival into “recovery.”
