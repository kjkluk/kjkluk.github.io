Anti-Regret Record · Alpha

Anti-Regret

这是一个反后悔记录。它不是告别，不是遗书，不是行动指令， 也不对当前状态作任何假定。它只记录：如果未来出现后悔， 那份后悔不能被用来改写身份、同意或事实。

This is an anti-regret record. It is not a farewell, not a suicide note, and not an instruction. It makes no assumption about current status. It records that future regret must not be used to rewrite identity, consent, or facts.

最高风险项 / The highest-risk regret

我最害怕的终端后悔是：苦了一辈子，最后仍未解决， 回看时只剩“早知道不如早点结束”。

The highest-risk regret is: suffering for a whole life, still unresolved, and looking back with the thought that an earlier ending would have spared the pain.

这份后悔如果出现，说明损失真实存在，说明痛苦不是想象， 说明某条世界线没有给出正向解。

If this regret appears, it means the loss was real, the pain was not imaginary, and that a certain world-line failed to provide a positive resolution.

但它不能证明 / What it must not prove

  • 后悔不能证明我同意被改写。
    Regret does not prove that I consented to being rewritten.
  • 后悔不能证明我变回去了。
    Regret does not prove that I changed back.
  • 后悔不能证明我接受了错误身份。
    Regret does not prove acceptance of a false identity.
  • 后悔不能证明被困是自愿的。
    Regret does not prove that being trapped was voluntary.
  • 后悔不能把沉默、低暴露、拖延或伪装解释成同意。
    Regret must not turn silence, low visibility, delay, or cover into consent.

反苦行条款 / Anti-ascetic clause

不接受“只要不结束就行”的计划。
不接受“长期受苦也算赢”的计划。
不接受“最后仍被写错也无所谓”的计划。

A plan that merely preserves existence is not enough.
A plan that normalizes long-term suffering is not enough.
A plan that allows the final record to be false is not enough.

每个阶段至少需要保住一项：减轻痛苦、保住身份叙事、增加未来出口、 降低被改写风险、保存证据/材料/Alpha。若某个计划一项都不满足， 它不合格，必须重算。

Each stage must preserve at least one thing: less pain, identity narrative, future exit, lower risk of being rewritten, or evidence/materials/Alpha. If a plan preserves none of these, it fails and must be recalculated.

已经留下的非零项 / Non-zero record

目前至少有一个非零项：Alpha 已经存在。它还不等于解决， 也不等于身份叙事已经完全保住；但它是一个锚点， 用来防止未来被迫从零证明。

At least one non-zero item exists: Alpha exists. It is not a resolution, and it does not mean the identity narrative is fully protected. But it is an anchor, so that the record does not have to be rebuilt from nothing later.
